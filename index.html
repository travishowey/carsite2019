Our History

Mike's Auto Mart was founded in 1453 by Sir Mikolous III. I know what you're thinking, "Cars weren't even invented then!" and you're right! Sir Mikolous III was such a visionary that he knew someday we would outgrow horses and carraiges and replace them with a better mode of transportation. After hundreds of years of waiting, Sir Mikolous III was proven correct, and his auto mart has flourished ever since. His great-great-great-great-great-great-great-great-great-great-great-great-grandson Mikolous XVIII now runs the shop with his son Mikolous XIX. They love helping people find the right car at the right price, so come on in and get the car you want for the price you deserve!
